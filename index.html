Who We Are

The Coaching Company Built by Amazon Sellers, for Amazon Sellers

Abuv The Par was founded on a simple premise: the best coaching comes from people who have actually done the work. Our founders — Stemar Greene, Tayeb Rahim, and Shofi Ahad — built their own seven-figure Amazon FBA wholesale operation before ever coaching a single student.

That real-world experience is the foundation of everything we teach. Our curriculum is not recycled YouTube content or generic eCommerce theory. It is the exact playbook we used to scale our own business — refined through years of testing, iteration, and coaching hundreds of entrepreneurs to their own results.

Based in New York, we serve a global community of Amazon sellers at every stage — from complete beginners to established sellers looking to break through to their next revenue milestone.
